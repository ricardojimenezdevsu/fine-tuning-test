Q: What is the Richardson Maturity Model?
A: The Richardson Maturity Model is a model used to consider the degree of coupling between the producer and consumer when choosing between REST and RPC models.
WA1: The Richardson Maturity Model is a model used for versioning APIs.
WA2: The Richardson Maturity Model is a model used to analyze the reliability of an API.
WA3: The Richardson Maturity Model is a model used to assess the performance of an API.
