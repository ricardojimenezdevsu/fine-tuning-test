Q: What is the purpose of the Microsoft REST API Guidelines?
A: The guidelines provide developers with terminology to determine whether requirements are optional or mandatory.
WA1: To provide developers with guidelines to create APIs.
WA2: To standardize the design of APIs.
WA3: To provide developers with a source of open standards.
