Q: At what point should the team create an ADR?
A: After there has been discussion ahead of the ADR and the record is a result of collective thinking in the team.
WA1: Before any discussion has taken place.
WA2: After the ADR has been published to the wider community.
WA3: When the engineering team has proposed the ADR.

Q: What is the purpose of publishing an ADR to the wider community?
A: To allow the opportunity for feedback beyond the immediate team.
WA1: To allow for discussion about the ADR.
WA2: To keep the ADR immutable.
WA3: To capture the change in perspective.
