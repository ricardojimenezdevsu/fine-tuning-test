Q: What is eastwest traffic?
A: Eastwest traffic can be thought of as service-to-service style of communication within a group of applications.
WA1: Eastwest traffic is communication between two machines.
WA2: Eastwest traffic is communication between two users.
WA3: Eastwest traffic is communication between two networks.
