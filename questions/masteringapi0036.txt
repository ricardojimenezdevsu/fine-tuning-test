Q: What is the Richardson Maturity Model?
A: A model presented by Leonard Richardson of different levels of adoption teams apply to building APIs from a REST perspective. 
WA1: A model presented by Martin Fowler of different levels of adoption teams apply to building APIs from a REST perspective. 
WA2: A model presented by Leonard Richardson of different levels of adoption teams apply to building APIs from a SOAP perspective. 
WA3: A model presented by Martin Fowler of different levels of adoption teams apply to building APIs from a SOAP perspective.
