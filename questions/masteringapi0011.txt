Q: Does using code from OReilly books require permission?
A: Selling or distributing examples from OReilly books does require permission.
WA1: No permission is needed.
WA2: Attribution is necessary.
WA3: No permission is needed but attribution is required.

Q: Is attribution usually required when using OReilly code?
A: We appreciate, but generally do not require, attribution.
WA1: No attribution is necessary.
WA2: Attribution is necessary.
WA3: Attribution is optional.
