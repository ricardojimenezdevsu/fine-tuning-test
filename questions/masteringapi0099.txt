Q: Why should end-to-end testing be done?
A: To ensure that services are still serving requests within targeted SLOs and that no sudden lag has been introduced.
WA1: To make development time easier.
WA2: To ensure that all security measures are in place.
WA3: To make sure that hardware is representative.

Q: What tools can be used for performance testing?
A: Gatling, JMeter, Locust, and K6.
WA1: Apache Web Server.
WA2: Microsoft Access.
WA3: Notepad.
